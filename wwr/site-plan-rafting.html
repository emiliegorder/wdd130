Overview

Purpose

This website is here to provide information to guests about the services we provide, pricing. We want to convince them to book a rafting trip with us and provide them a way to both book and pay online.

Audiance

Who all is welcome to come rafting with us? We allow rafters aged 13 to 17 to come with us as long as they have a parent or gaurdian with them. They must sign a waver. Rafters aged 18 to 60 are all welcome, target demographic is closer to 20-40, they still need to sign a waver. What are they coming to this site for? They are here to see if they want to go rafting, if they think it would be fun and safe, looking for a reasonably priced activity to get them outside and feeling adrenaline. How will my audiance be accessing my site? Chances are their first interaction with the site will be on a phone as they casually glance at rafting as a possibility. If we catch their eye they may come back on a computer for real research and potential booking. What do they expect to find on the website? Dates and times available to book, pricing, age limits, waivers, locations, contact services, frequently asked questions, online booking.
