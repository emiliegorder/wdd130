Purpose

Provide easy access to booking rafting trips with us, provide all necessary information about times, saftey, and payment for curious minds.

Audiance

The target audiance is adults and older teenagers who are would be booking their rafting trip through us. They will expect to find scheduling and payment information as well as safety q and a's. Chances are they are most likely to be planning this trip in advance are will probably use a laptop or desktop computer.
